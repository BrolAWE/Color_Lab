Research and Educational Center of the Smolensk State University ColorLab is studying the principles of color harmony in urbane environments.

In this experiment, we want to find out what color combinations you personally like.

This study takes about 10 minutes. We will show you 5 color charts. Each chart consists of three horizontal stripes of different widths. The colors of the top and bottom stripes are fixed and you cannot change them. You will be asked to select a color for the white (uncolored) stripe in the middle. Choose this color so that the whole color combination is as harmonious as possible.

If you are ready to take part in this study, please, fill out the form below.
